Taking Gender Equity to the Streets

A project completed by Kerin Grewal and Stephanie Shin

Curated by The Mayor's Office of Women's Advancement at the City of Boston

Representation is one of MOWA's priority areas. We recognize that male names and pronouns are often the default and given Boston's long parochial history, we want to make sure our City represents its current demographics (52% women). That said, we'd also like to know if there are streets that are good candidates for re-naming (repetitive, etc.).

In order to find the best possible candidates for re-naming. We analyzed the following factors/datasets:
  • The street names in Boston and their genders
  • Boston landmarks and their locations/street names
  • Boston public schools and their locations/street names
  • Uber data within Boston
  • ... And more!

You are now able to see an interactive map where you can filter by neighborhood and view the different streets that are good candidates for renaming.
